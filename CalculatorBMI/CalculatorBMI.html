What are Calories?

Calories, by googles definition, are a unit of energy defined as the amount of heat needed to raise the temperature of a quantity of water by one degree Celsius.

Now, in layman's terms, calories are the way we attempt to calculate how much energy our bodies need to do the work that it does. We all know that we get "energy" from food, but how much "energy" do we really need?
